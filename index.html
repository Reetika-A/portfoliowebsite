About me!

My name is Reetika Acharya. I am from Rourkela, Odisha. Rourkela, also known as the Steel City of Odisha, is famous for its steel industry and vibrant culture. Growing up in Rourkela has instilled in me a deep appreciation for its rich heritage and industrious spirit. I have completed both my matriculation and intermediate from Delhi Public School, Rourkela, which provided me with not only a strong academic foundation but also taught me invaluable life lessons. Currently I'm pursuing my Bachelor of Technology degree from VSSUT,Burla

My Interests!

Outside the realm of academics, I have a diverse set of interests that bring joy and fulfillment to my life. I am an avid badminton enthusiast and enjoy both watching and playing the sport. I love crocheting too. Crocheting has been a source of creative expression for me, allowing me to channel my thoughts and emotions into beautiful and intricate designs. Crocheting is a very peaceful activity. It helps to calm a person's mind. Crocheting is known to increase concentration too. Apart from badminton and crocheting, I am passionate about various other hobbies, including cooking, crafting, reading books, and singing.
